Who Am I

Well Hello, I'm Harrison

I'm a Kenyan, a country where mobile money has become the banner of consumer economy. I practice coding primarily to change my world and assimilate technology in the numerous endeavors set to curb the social, economic and political problems that plague our land. In addition, I attend Python and JavaScript meet-ups more often. I also write blogs and practise data analysis.
