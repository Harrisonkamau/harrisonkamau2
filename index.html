Frequently Asked Questions

What made you want to become a Web Developer?

Simply put, I love to create! Ever since I can remember I've been painting, drawing, sewing and figuring out how to make one thing or another. Pairing my love for art with my interest in computers, Web Development was just a natural progression for me. The more I learn, the more my passion for this industry grows.

What's your design process?

I imagine my process for Web Design will be much the same as it is for freelance art work I've done. I believe fully in collaboration with the client, and listening carefully to your needs. I understand how nerve wrecking it can be to entrust a such an important job to someone you hardly know, and want to make sure the client is always a hundred comfortable comfortable. The process for design generally starts with a discussion regarding what sort of image you'd like to convey, your desired audience and your own personal ideas. Inspirations are welcome! Feel free to put together a collection of what you like. And if you have no idea, we'll work together to make sure the final product is perfect!

What is your turnaround time?

This is fully dependant on project size, content and how well you know what you'd like the final outcome to be. Please let me know if you have a specific deadline, and I will ensure I meet it. Otherwise, we can discuss finish times after we've worked out what exactly the project entails. That way a final project can be achieved in a timeframe which we are both comfortable with.

How will we communicate, if we're not in the same city?

We can communicate in a multitude of different ways; Skype, email and phone are the most common. I generally prefer not to text message with clients, as it isn't an effective way of communicating for business purposes. If you have a preffered method, please let me know; I'm fully flexible to client needs. Alternately, if you are unsure how to use a method (such as Skype), don't be shy in telling me! I'm happy to help you get started. As far as a means of content transfer (picture files, etc.), any large files can be sent to me using a dropbox link I will provide.
